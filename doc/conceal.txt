*conceal.txt* conceal


Vim plugin to hide a pattern from the buffer.


COMMANDS                                                         *conceal.vim*
==============================================================================

  *:Conceal* [!] [pattern]

Hide the given [pattern]. With <bang>, clear specific patterns, or all
patterns if none given.
