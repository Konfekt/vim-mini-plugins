USAGE                                                              *ctrlg.vim*
==============================================================================

It remaps <C-G> and g<C-G> in normal mode, replacing |CTRL-G| and |g_CTRL-G|.

You can add support to your language by creating a variable:
>
  let g:ctrlg_lang = ['Modified', 'lines', 'Not a file.', '[No Name]']
<
and you should translate those strings into your own language.
